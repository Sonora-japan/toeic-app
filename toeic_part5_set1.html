TOEIC Part 5 - Set 1: Current News Topics

Choose the word or phrase that best completes each sentence.

1. Following the widespread flooding in Southeast Asia, international aid organizations rapidly ________ emergency supplies to affected regions.

A) deployed

B) deploying

C) to deploy

D) deploy
