【Part 5 - 語彙】

Shohei Ohtani’s first home run of the 2025 season was ______ after a video review at the Tokyo Dome.

  1. awarded
  2. confirmed ✅
  3. announced
  4. received

→ 「確認された」が自然。ビデオ判定の文脈では "confirmed" を使います。

【Part 6 - 短文穴埋め】

[Official MLB Memo]
Shohei Ohtani hit a two-run homer that was initially questioned but later ______ valid by the video replay system.

  1. considered
  2. remained
  3. declared
  4. found ✅

→ find A B = AをBだと判断する。典型的なTOEIC語法。
